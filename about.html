QCITS was born during a pandemic. What started as a small group of coders solving problems remotely became a full-service IT solutions company trusted by businesses across Sri Lanka.

Who We Are

Quarantine Coders
IT Solutions

We are a team of engineers, designers, and strategists who came together in 2020 with one shared belief: that great software changes lives. What began as a lockdown project quickly grew into a company that businesses rely on for their most important digital challenges.

From small startups launching their first product to established companies modernising their systems, we bring the same obsession with quality to every project. We don't just write code — we solve problems.

Based in Sri Lanka, we work globally. Our clients span e-commerce, healthcare, logistics, hospitality, and finance.
